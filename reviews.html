Is this a real payment system?

This is a frontend demo checkout. It can later connect to Paystack, Flutterwave, or bank transfer confirmation.

Can products be changed?

Yes. Product names, prices, descriptions, and images can be edited inside app.js.

Does the cart work?

Yes. The cart works across pages using browser localStorage.

Can it become a full website?

Yes. A backend, database, admin dashboard, and order management system can be added later.
